# Vision

**Strategic direction for Fernandes Labs.**

## North Star

Building a portfolio of high-quality web products with shared standards, discoverable by users who need them.

## Principles

1. **Quality First**: Every product must meet our quality standards before release
2. **Shared Foundation**: Reuse code, patterns, and knowledge across products
3. **Discovery Optimized**: SEO and accessibility are built in, not added later
4. **Sustainable Growth**: Each product benefits from work on previous products

## Product Philosophy

### Focus
Each product solves one problem well. No feature creep or scope expansion after launch.

### Standards
All products pass the same quality gate: lint, build, tests, Lighthouse, accessibility, SEO, security.

### Branding
All products are Fernandes Labs products. Consistent experience across the portfolio.

## Long-term Goals

### 50 Products
- Standardized templates eliminate setup time
- Shared components reduce maintenance
- Automated QA catches regressions
- Documentation enables fast onboarding

### 100 Products
- Sub-teams organized by product category
- Dedicated product managers
- External contributor program
- Commercial licensing options

### Scale Considerations

As we grow:
- Standards become more critical
- Automation replaces manual QA
- Architecture decisions compound
- Technical debt multiplies

Every decision made today must account for 10x scale tomorrow.

## Success Metrics

- Products shipping monthly
- Lighthouse scores > 95 average
- Zero accessibility violations
- SEO traffic growth
- User retention

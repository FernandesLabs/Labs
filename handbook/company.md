# Company

**About Fernandes Labs.**

## Mission

Build a portfolio of high-quality web products with shared standards.

## Values

### Quality First
Every product must meet our quality standards before release. We prioritize long-term reputation over short-term shipping.

### Simplicity
Products solve one problem well. No feature creep. No unnecessary complexity.

### Accessibility
All users must be able to access our products. WCAG AA compliance is mandatory.

### Transparency
We publish source code, document decisions, and welcome feedback.
